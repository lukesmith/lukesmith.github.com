What are Sony playing at?

Taking a page from Microsoft's launch, the PS3 will support a hard drive, but
it may be something you have to buy seperately. To make matters much, much, much,
insanely worse, the 60 GB hard drive will be REQUIRED to play games. That's right,
you may be able to buy a PS3 that doesn't actually play games. Go figure.

So if this is true, and the only other reason for having a PS3 is for its media capability,
you would be buying a big box of the latest technology and not be able to make the
most of it.
