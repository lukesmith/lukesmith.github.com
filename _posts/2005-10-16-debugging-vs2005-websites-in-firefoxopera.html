When I first got hold of Visual Studio 2005 beta2 and created a webapp I tried copying
the built in webservers url to firefox and opera to see how the thing rendered, which
never worked. And I only figured this out today.

  1. Select the property pages of the website in the solution explorer then select 'Start
    Options'
  2. under the 'Server' section uncheck 'NTLM Authentication'

    Now instead of needing to upload your website to your server you can do it locally.
