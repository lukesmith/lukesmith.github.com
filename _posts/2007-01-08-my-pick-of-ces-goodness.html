I'm shattered, I stayed up like a good geek and watched Bill Gates CES (Consumer Electronics
Show) Keynote. The keynote being at 6:30pm Pacific time making it 2:30am my time.

A few cool things I can't wait for from the keynote:

  • Windows Ultimate Extras - well mainly just
    the one makes me go 'ooo' and I like things which make me go 'ooo'. Windows DreamScene allows
    you to play a high-def video (mpeg or wmv) as your desktop background. During
    the keynote the background was a pretty high def image of a waterfall. Then the presenter
    enabled DreamScene and
    the background came to life with flowing water 'ooo'. It will probably look a
    bit poopy with your own videos as you will see the loop but Microsoft is shipping
    it with looping videos. Now it is kind of a bit pointless, but none the less don't
    let that take anything away from the coolness. Watch the on10 video
    with info on all the Extras.
  • Windows Home Server -
    This excites me more than the 'ooo' above, mainly because it has a very practical
    use. I've just ran out of storage on Goofy (my desktop pc) including the 260GB external
    HDD so I'm going to need more storage (I was going to build a file server with Server
    2003 but can't afford it quite yet). Well Microsoft has the answer to home users who
    have more than one PC and wishes to easily manage all of their media (pictures, videos,
    music, files) allowing access over the internet via a friendly URL. As well as backing
    up your whole computer (thats every bit on the HDD). on10 video
    of WHS.
  • IPTV coming to the Xbox360 - Record and watch TV on your Xbox360. Chat to friends
    while watching tv using Xbox Live, or record shows while your playing online. on10 video

Go watch the keynote.
