Just over 2 years after I dreamed up Ambulex, which then evolved into Saviour, it
has now been created by another company. The whole idea of equipping paramedics with
PDAs, allowing them to keep in contact with the command center while in the Ambulance
as well as away from the ambulance. The whole thing was dreamed up one day by me and
brought to life with the rest of Team Babbage during the Imagine Cup 2004.

Everything in their product mentioned in the article was
part of Ambulex. How long until they expand it to become Saviour?
