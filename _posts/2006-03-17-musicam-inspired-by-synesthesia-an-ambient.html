It’s
internal examiner day #1 for my uni mates today, showing off their final year projects.


 

Derek has
made a cool HCI webcam application, which plays different sounds depending on which
colors are present in the image. You can even play some little tunes on it if you
can get the timing right.


 

Check out musicam.co.uk for
information on his project. It will be updated over the weekend with pictures and
videos.


 

Chris Tingley,
of etingley and Macboy fame, also has an excellent
augmented reality project which unfortunately I’ve not seen yet. I’m sure
he will be putting up videos and photos of it in the near future.
